Thank you for your interest in the Performance Co-Pilot conference!

If you would like to join our group of presenters, we would love to hear from you.

You are encouraged to present on new development, production system deployment and anaylsis with PCP, or provide a hands-on tutorial.

Talks on technologies closely related to PCP, such as Vector , Grafana and Redis are also entirely appropriate, as are topics related to the PCP organisations involvement in the Google Summer of Code program.

Slots of 15, 30 and 45 minutes are available and all presenters are guaranteed registration.

To register your interest in presenting, please send an email to the organising committee members to discuss further.

This is a highly targetted and deeply technical conference. Advertising or marketing style presentations would be unsuitable.
