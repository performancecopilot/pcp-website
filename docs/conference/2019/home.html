Where? When?

125 Flinders Lane, Melbourne
The Odd One Out

Friday 1st March
9am - 5pm

This year's Performance Co-Pilot conference will be held in Melbourne, Australia!

The conference brings together developers and performance analysts to collaborate on current and future PCP developments.

Presentations and discussion will focus on both core PCP technologies, as well as closely related projects such as Vector , Grafana and Redis

Your attendence is free of charge thanks to our wonderful sponsors however we ask that all attendees register to ensure the venue can cater for everyone.

Please read the the Linux Foundation events Code of Conduct which we will use once again for this conference.
